## 🎚️ GUIDING PRINCIPLES

1. **No Compilations** — Unless they meet the *exceptions*.
2. **Condition First** — Prioritize sealed or NM vinyl; VG+ only if rare or historically relevant.
3. **Pressing Matters** — Prefer original analog or reputable remaster (MoFi, Rhino, Analogue Productions).
4. **No Steely Dan Rule** — *Aja* and similar "engineering showcase" albums are excluded unless found cheap and clean.
5. **Complements, Not Clutter** — Add only records that balance genres or fill historical gaps.
6. **Sonic Relevance** — Every record must *bump* or *breathe* on the actual playback chain.
7. **Duplicate Avoidance** — No aesthetic variants unless mastering differs.
8. **Digital Exceptions** — Post-CD-era titles allowed only if worth archival FLAC.
9. **Emotional Rule** — If it doesn't move you, it doesn't belong.
