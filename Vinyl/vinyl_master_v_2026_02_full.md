## Strategic Collection Guidance

### Five Rule Strategy

1. **No “Greatest Hits.”** Focus on single-artist, studio-defined albums.
2. **Pre-1995 or post-2010 only.** Avoid 1995–2010 “digital purgatory.”
3. **Used preferred.** Prioritize Capricorn, Columbia, Warner, and Asylum pressings when feasible.
4. **Modern vinyl intent.** Favor modern artists who master/mix with vinyl in mind (e.g., Taylor Swift, Post Malone, Chris Stapleton, Silk Sonic).
5. **Compilation / Greatest Hits / 'Digital Purgatory' exceptions (allowed when):**

   * **Emotional value** (e.g., *Sublime*, *The Marshall Mathers LP 2*, etc.)
   * **Critically acclaimed film soundtracks**, live albums or collaborations
   * Artists I'm **unlikely to buy full studio discographies** from
