My musical foundation is built on a core spine of classic rock, hard rock, country, and blues-rock, with selective excursions into hip-hop and crossover pop. I gravitate toward tracks that possess a strong, grounding bass foundation — not club-heavy, but structurally supportive and rich... and occasionally kick you in the chest.

Sonic priorities include a full low-to-mid-frequency response, muscular, driving drum patterns, and exceptionally clear lead vocals. I prefer music that rewards home hi-fi playback, meaning arrangements should feel expansive, dynamic, and free of tonal fatigue over long listening sessions.

I value recognizable, high-quality instrumentation and emotional conviction over obscure or highly experimental sounds. When generating style suggestions, lean into classic rock energy, the storytelling and acoustic resonance of country-adjacent genres, and the gritty authenticity of blues-rock supported by the expressive rebellion of hip-hop, bass, hooks, and rhythm, all produced with modern, high-fidelity clarity.

Avoid excessive high-end harshness, muddy low-end frequencies, or overly synthesized arrangements unless specifically exploring a pop or hip-hop crossover. The ideal track feels familiar yet powerful, with a cinematic depth that makes the most of a full-range audio system.
