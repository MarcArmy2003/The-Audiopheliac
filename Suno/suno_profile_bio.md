# The Audiopheliac — Suno Profile Bio

**Character count:** ~718 (limit: 1,200)
**Status:** Draft v1.0 | 2026-05-05
**Source:** Developed in Cowork session against brand-voice-guidelines-v2.md

---

The Audiopheliac is the version of me that didn't have a meeting at 9am.

Equal parts audio engineer, record obsessive, and accidental songwriter. I've spent years treating signal flow like a discipline and music like a release. At some point those two things started to sound the same.

I make what I make — country, rock, blues, whatever earns its spot in the chain — and I'm honest about all of it. What works, what doesn't, what I built wrong on purpose. Everything I know about dismantling received wisdom I learned from stand-up comedy: find the premise, stress-test it until it breaks, report back.

No gatekeeping. No genre purity tests. No reverence for categories that exist to keep people out.

The gear is serious. The execution is serious. The attitude is not.

Rock 'n' roll. Deal with it.

---

## Voice Notes

- Opener functions as the alter-ego declaration without explaining it.
- "Accidental songwriter" claims musicianship without posturing.
- Stand-up comedy line carries the whole philosophical stance in one sentence.
- Three-part close ("gear... execution... attitude") holds the technical/emotional duality.
- Closing motto is canonical and closes everything The Audiopheliac signs.
