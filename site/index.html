The Audiopheliac

Intelligent Audio Systems

A unified music intelligence system connecting local FLAC libraries, Spotify data, and vinyl collections into one cohesive experience.

System Overview

The Audiopheliac is a personal music operating system. It bridges the gap between ownership and streaming, providing insight into what you listen to, what you own, and what you are missing.

Core Capabilities

  • Local library indexing and analysis
  • Spotify integration and synchronization
  • Ownership gap detection
  • Collection intelligence and acquisition targeting

Current Status

  • 6,823 local tracks indexed
  • Spotify library integrated
  • Cross-source matching engine active
  • Gap analysis operational
